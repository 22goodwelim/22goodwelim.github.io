Our Mission

Our mission here at Sailing Chocho is to simply improve the world and coincidently, ourselves. We have traveled to many places throughout the world and we have seen first hand the problems that we as humans have created. The pollution in the oceans, overfishing, dead coral (for more information please see our conservation page), and much more. We have seen how we have damaged the Earth to near extinction in some ways. We are dedicated to sharing this destruction and how we can prevent this. Through this, we are hoping to improve ourselves as well. As we travel we are hoping to discover new and amazing things whether that be people and their different cultures, amazing landscapes that have hardly been traversed, or ideas that we pick up along our journey.
